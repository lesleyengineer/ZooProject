Our Elephant Sanctuary
In our sanctuary we have 4 African elephants
They are very friendly and love visitors so make sure to visit Jungle River where you will find them
If you come between 1pm and 2pm you will catch them having lunch!

Monkey Mountain
There are currently over 25 differnt species of monkey on Monkey Mountain!
We have everything from mandrills, to marmosets and even a snow monkey
Make sure to pop down to Tropical Bay to see how many you can spot!

Dolphin Bay
Our dolphins have all been rescued and saved after having been damaged by fishing equipment
We release them back into the wild as soon as they have recovered and are ready to be back on their own
Come and visit them at Little Beach and see how our team helps them with their recovery!
